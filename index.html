About Us

Our company was founded nearly 20 years ago in 2008 by Jodi Mapel. Jodi has always had a passion for driving and teaching. She decided to put two of her passions together and founded Astar Driving School.

Our Cars

We drive Mustangs and Dodge Chargers. Not only are they cool and American made, but more importantly they are safe. Our cars are meticulously maintained and cleaned. Rest easy knowing that you or your child will be safe behind the wheel of our cars.

Our Method

Each lesson with Astar Driving School is one on one. We do not "piggy-back" as we believe students will learn better without the distraction of another student in the car. We teach students how to be safe on the road and not just how to pass a driving test. One on one instruction in real world circumstances.
